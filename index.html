Common Questions

Do you bring your own equipment?+
Yes — we arrive fully equipped with all professional tools and eco-friendly products. You don't need to provide a thing.
How do I get a price?+
Fill in our quote form — tell us about your property and upload some photos. Pankaj will personally review and send you a tailored quote within 2 hours. No price lists, no guesswork.
Do you guarantee deposit return?+
We offer a 72-hour reclean guarantee. If your landlord isn't satisfied, we return and reclean the flagged areas — completely free.
Are you available at weekends?+
Yes — 7 days a week, 8am to 8pm. Weekend and same-day bookings subject to availability.
How do I pay?+
Bank transfer, cash or card on the day. A small deposit may be required for large bookings.
Do you work with letting agents?+
Yes — we work closely with landlords and letting agents across Surrey for regular property turnovers. Trade rates available for multiple properties.
